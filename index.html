Churro Bundt Cake

This Churro Bundt Cake is a sweet treat you must have on your do nothing days at home.
an easy, quick, and delicous dessert that you can enjoy with the whole family.

Ingredients

Ingredient Amounts
Sour cream 1/2 cup
Egg 1 (large)
Vanilla extract 1 1/2 teaspoons
Vegetable oil 2 tablespoons
Melted Butter (unsalted) 2 tablespoons
Sugar 1/2 cup
All purpose flour 1 cup
Baking soda 1/2 teaspoon
Pinch of salt Not to much

Ingredients for Topping
